Translation of hello – English–Arabic dictionary

hello

exclamation
uk
/helˈəʊ/ us
/heˈloʊ/
A1
said when you meet someone or start talking with someone
أهلاً وسَهْلاً / مَرْحَباً
Hello, Christina. How are you?
A1
used when you start speaking on the phone
ألو
Hello, this is Alex.

(Translation of hello from the Cambridge English-Arabic Dictionary © Cambridge University Press)
